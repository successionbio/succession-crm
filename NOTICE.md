## What Succession CRM Adds

Succession CRM extends Twenty CRM with features purpose-built for life science sales and marketing teams:

- Proprietary life science company and people database integration
- AI-powered skills for lead discovery, qualification, enrichment, and outreach
- Event intelligence and signal monitoring for life science conferences and triggers
- Marketing automation integration (Mautic)
- Scheduling integration (Cal.com)
- Company profile context engine for ICP and persona management
